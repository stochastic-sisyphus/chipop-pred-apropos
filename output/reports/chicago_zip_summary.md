### Neighborhood Typology and Development Patterns

#### 1. Established Growth Areas
- **Examples**: 60610, 60606, 60603
- **Characteristics**: High-income, strong amenities, consistent growth
- **Strategy**: Luxury and premium housing focus, mixed-use development

#### 2. Transitional/Gentrifying Areas
- **Examples**: 60607, 60622, 60647
- **Characteristics**: Rapid permit activity, moderate income growth
- **Strategy**: Mixed-income development, preserve affordability, add amenities

#### 3. Emerging Growth Opportunities
- **Examples**: 60617, 60636, 60651
- **Characteristics**: Above-average growth, below-average permit activity
- **Strategy**: Early investment opportunity, focus on infrastructure and amenities

#### 4. Stable Residential Areas
- **Examples**: 60625, 60630, 60646
- **Characteristics**: Moderate growth, moderate permit activity
- **Strategy**: Maintain neighborhood character, focus on housing quality

#### 5. Underperforming Areas
- **Examples**: 60621, 60624, 60633
- **Characteristics**: Below-average growth, very low permit activity
- **Strategy**: Public investment needed, address barriers to development

### Methodology Notes
- Population growth represents annual average percentage change (2013-2023)
- Permit count represents total building permits issued (2013-2023)
- Economic sensitivity based on permit reduction during economic stress periods
- Projections based on Random Forest regression model using historical data
